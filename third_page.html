Origin of YajurVeda

The Yajurveda is believed to have been composed around the same time as the Rigveda, between 1500 BCE and 1200 BCE. It is primarily a collection of prose mantras (Yajus) used by priests (Adhvaryu) during rituals and sacrificial ceremonies (Yajnas). The Yajurveda is divided into two main parts: the "White Yajurveda" (Shukla Yajurveda) and the "Black Yajurveda" (Krishna Yajurveda).
